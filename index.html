Our Story

From family kitchen to your favorite table

Saffron Kitchen & Bar started with a simple dream — to bring the bold, authentic flavors of Indian cuisine to Atlanta in a way that feels both familiar and refreshingly new.

Every dish begins with recipes passed down through three generations, reimagined with modern technique and the freshest local ingredients. We don't just cook food — we craft experiences.

— Chef Arjun & Family
